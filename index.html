D'AULAIRES' BOOK OF GREEK MYTHS

by Eric Lemaire

This chart represents the relationships between the 12 major gods and goddesses of Mt. Olympus.
Here you see all the myths of these gods and the gods that are mentioned within these myths.
It tells a story of not only the gods and their importance, but their relationship with others.

In D'Aulaires' Book of Greek Mythology, each god is given their own myth.
However, often times, other gods will appear in these myths because they share a relationship or
affiliation. This visualization is designed to explore those relationships and see what connections
we can derive from these stories and who appears with the most frequency, such as Zeus,
and those with less frequency, like Hestia, who doesn't have a myth at all.
